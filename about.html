🏥 Our Story

Deeksha Dental Care was established with a simple but powerful vision: to make high-quality dental care accessible to every patient in Bikaner and the surrounding region of Rajasthan. We recognized that dental health is often neglected due to fear, lack of awareness, or cost concerns — and we set out to change that.

Located conveniently near Eid Gah Bari in Bikaner, our clinic was built from the ground up with patient comfort as the central priority. From our welcoming environment to our painless treatment protocols, every aspect of your visit has been thoughtfully designed to eliminate dental anxiety and replace it with confidence and trust.

Over the years, we have grown from a general dental practice to a comprehensive multi-specialty clinic offering root canal treatments, dental implants, orthodontics (braces and aligners), prosthetics (dentures, post & core, crowns), surgical extractions, cosmetic dentistry, pediatric dental care, and preventive dentistry — all under one roof.

We are proud to have served thousands of families across Bikaner, Rajasthan, becoming the go-to dental clinic for complex cases that previously required patients to travel to larger cities like Jaipur or Jodhpur.

🔬 Our Equipment & Technology

We invest in modern dental technology to deliver better outcomes with less discomfort and shorter treatment times:

  • Digital X-Ray (RVG): 80% less radiation than conventional X-rays. Instant, high-resolution images for accurate diagnosis.
  • Electronic Apex Locator: Precise root canal length measurement for optimal RCT outcomes.
  • Rotary NiTi Endodontic System: Faster, more predictable root canal shaping with flexible instruments.
  • Ultrasonic Scaler: Professional teeth cleaning that removes calculus effectively without damaging enamel.
  • LED Curing Light: Fast polymerization for composite fillings — stronger, longer-lasting restorations.
  • Surgical Motor for Implants: Precise torque-controlled implant placement for optimal osseointegration.
  • Autoclave Sterilizer: Class B sterilization ensuring complete pathogen elimination between patients.
