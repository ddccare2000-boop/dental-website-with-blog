Home → Blog → Dant Dard ke Upay

Dant dard achanak aa jaata hai — raat ko, holiday mein, ya jab dentist band ho. Yeh 5 ghar ke upay temporary relief denge. Par yaad rakho: yeh dard ka ilaaj nahi, sirf aaram hai — root cause ke liye dentist zaroor jaao.

⚠️ Pehle yeh padho: Agar dant dard ke saath chehra sooji hai, bukhar hai, ya saas lene mein takleef hai — yeh dental emergency hai. Turant hospital ya dentist ke paas jaao. Yeh infection serious ho sakta hai.
1

🧄 Lahsun (Garlic) — Sabse Powerful

India ka #1 traditional remedy — aur scientifically proven bhi

✅ Science Approved

Lahsun mein Allicin compound hota hai jo powerful natural antibacterial aur anti-inflammatory agent hai. Research mein yeh dental bacteria ke against effective paya gaya hai — including Streptococcus mutans jo cavity banata hai.

🔪 Kaise use karein:

  1. 1-2 lahsun ki kali lo — fresh, raw
  2. Halka crush karo ya chaa lo jab tak rass nikle
  3. Seedha dard wale daant par rakho — 2-3 minute
  4. Din mein 2-3 baar repeat karo
  5. Baad mein kulli kar lo — smell ke liye
⚠️ Kuch logon ko lahsun se gum irritation ho sakti hai. 5 minute se zyaada mat rakho. Agar burning badh jaaye toh hata lo.
2

🧂 Namak Paani Kulli — Classic Remedy

Grandma ka nushkha + doctor ki salah — dono ek saath

✅ Science Approved

Warm salt water rinse ek proven antiseptic solution hai. Yeh gum ki sujan kam karta hai, bacteria reduce karta hai, aur wound healing mein help karta hai. ADA (American Dental Association) bhi yeh recommend karti hai.

🥄 Kaise banayein:

  1. 1 glass gunguna paani (lukewarm — hot nahi)
  2. Usme ½ teaspoon namak mila lo
  3. 30 seconds tak achhe se kulli karo
  4. Thuk do — peeyo mat
  5. Din mein 3-4 baar karo — khana khane ke baad bhi
⚠️ Bahut zyaada namak mat dalo — concentrated salt solution gums ko irritate karta hai. ½ tsp per glass enough hai.

🌿 Laung (Clove) — Dentist ka Secret

Dentists khud clove oil use karte hain procedures mein

✅ Dentist Approved

Laung mein Eugenol hota hai — yeh natural anesthetic aur antibacterial compound hai. Dentists actually procedures mein eugenol-based products use karte hain. Ghar mein laung ya clove oil se temporary numbing milti hai.

🌿 Kaise use karein (3 tarike):

  1. Seedha laung: 1 laung ko dard wale daant ke paas rakho — thoda dabao. 15-20 min.
  2. Clove oil: Cotton ball pe 2-3 drops — dard wali jagah pe dabao. (Gum pe seedha mat lagao)
  3. Laung powder: Thoda powder + thoda paani = paste — dard wali jagah pe lagao
⚠️ Clove oil seedha gums ya soft tissue pe zyaada mat lagao — burn ho sakta hai. Cotton ball se apply karo.
4

🧊 Cold Compress (Thandi Sikai)

Sujan aur dard dono ke liye — simple aur instant

✅ Science Approved

Cold compress blood vessels ko constrict karta hai jisse swelling aur nerve signals dono kam hote hain. Especially useful jab chehra thoda sooja ho ya trauma se dard ho (chot lagi ho).

🧊 Sahi tarika:

  1. Ice cubes ko ek kapde ya zip-lock bag mein bandh karo
  2. Seedhe skin ya gum pe ice mat lagao — frostbite ho sakta hai
  3. Chehere ke bahar — affected side pe — 15-20 minute rakho
  4. 15 minute break do — phir repeat karo
  5. Din mein 4-5 baar kar sakte ho
⚠️ Agar dant bahut sensitive hai aur thand se dard badhta hai — tab cold compress mat karo. Warm compress better rahega aapke liye.
5

💊 OTC Painkiller — Fastest Relief

Jab natural remedies kam na karein — safe medicines

✅ Medically Proven

Ghar pe available over-the-counter painkillers sabse fast aur reliable relief dete hain. Ibuprofen (Brufen, Combiflam) anti-inflammatory bhi hai — dard + sujan dono ke liye. Paracetamol (Crocin, Calpol) safer option hai agar stomach issue hai.

💊 Safe use guide:

  1. Ibuprofen 400mg (Brufen) — khana kha ke lo, har 6-8 ghante
  2. Paracetamol 500mg (Crocin) — safer, har 4-6 ghante
  3. Dono saath mat lo zyaada dose ke liye — dangerous hai
  4. 3 din se zyaada mat lo bina dentist se poochhe
  5. Aspirin seedha daant pe mat rakho — yeh purana myth hai aur gum burn karta hai
⚠️ Stomach problem ya ulcer hai toh Ibuprofen avoid karo. Pregnant hain toh koi bhi medicine lene se pehle doctor se poochho.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Kya Aspirin seedha daant pe rakhne se dard jaata hai?
Nahi — yeh ek common myth hai jo actually harmful hai. Aspirin tablet ko gum pe rakhne se chemical burn ho jaata hai aur munh ke tissue damage hote hain. Aspirin sirf khane se kaam karta hai — swallow karo, seedha mat lagao.
Raat ko dant dard zyaada kyun hota hai?
Raat ko dant dard zyaada feel hota hai kyunki leta hone pe blood pressure head ki taraf zyaada hota hai — is wajah se dard badhta hai. Doosri wajah yeh hai ki raat ko aur koi distraction nahi hoti toh dard pe zyaada dhyan jaata hai. Slightly uthha ke sona — ek pillow extra — thoda help karta hai.
Kya whisky ya alcohol dant dard mein help karta hai?
Thoda alcohol apply karna (cotton pad se) very mild numbing de sakta hai — laung se bahut kam. Peene se koi direct benefit nahi hai dant ke liye. Yeh ek ghar ka purana nushkha hai lekin scientifically clove oil ya cold compress zyaada effective hain. Bachon ke daanton par bilkul mat lagao.
Ghar ke upay kab tak reliable hain?
Maximum 2-3 din. Agar 3 din baad bhi dard hai — chahe ghar ke upay se temporary aaram mil raha ho — tab bhi dentist ke paas jaana chahiye. Dard temporary khatam hone ka matlab yeh nahi ki problem theek ho gayi. Infection andar badh sakta hai bina dard ke bhi.
