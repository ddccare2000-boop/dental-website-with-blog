Dental Implants at Deeksha Dental Care, Bikaner

A dental implant is a titanium screw placed into the jawbone to replace a missing tooth root. It fuses with bone and supports a crown, bridge or denture. Below is a comprehensive guide to implant types, stages, and all associated costs. updated 2026

📌 Factors That Influence Dental Implant Cost

  • Number of implants and need for bone grafting / sinus lift
  • Type of implant system (global brands like Nobel, Straumann vs. high‑quality Korean/Indian systems)
  • Material of the crown (zirconia vs. metal ceramic)
  • Experience of implantologist and clinic location
  • Diagnostic imaging (CBCT) and laboratory fees

Treatment Duration & Healing

Osseointegration: 3–6 months for lower jaw, 4–7 months for upper jaw. Immediate loading (teeth in a day) possible in selected cases with All‑on‑4. Full treatment from implant to final crown can take 4–8 months.

🔬 What Determines Implant Success?

🦴 Bone Quality & Quantity

Type II/III bone (moderately dense) offers best osseointegration. Low-quality bone (Type IV, common in upper back jaw) may require bone grafting. CBCT scan shows exact bone volume before surgery.

🚭 No Smoking

Smoking reduces blood flow to healing bone and increases implant failure risk by 2–3×. We strongly advise smoking cessation for at least 2 weeks pre-surgery and throughout healing.

💊 Medical Conditions

Controlled diabetes, hypertension, and thyroid conditions are manageable. Uncontrolled diabetes significantly impairs healing. Osteoporosis patients on bisphosphonates require special protocols.

🧼 Oral Hygiene

Peri-implantitis (implant gum disease) affects ~20% of implants at 10 years when hygiene is poor. Brush twice daily, use interdental brushes, and attend regular checkups to prevent it.

📋 Post-Implant Care — Critical Do's & Don'ts

✅ Do This

  • Apply ice packs to reduce swelling (20 min on/off)
  • Take prescribed antibiotics & pain medication
  • Eat soft foods for 2 weeks post-surgery
  • Rinse gently with warm salt water after meals
  • Sleep with head slightly elevated
  • Attend all follow-up appointments

❌ Avoid These

  • Smoking (at least 6–8 weeks post-surgery)
  • Touching the surgical site with tongue/fingers
  • Hard, crunchy, or chewy foods initially
  • Strenuous physical activity for 48–72 hours
  • Hot beverages immediately after surgery
  • Missing follow-up check appointments
