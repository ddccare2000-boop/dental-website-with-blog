Seedha jawab: Implant ka titanium screw lifelong chalta hai — 25-30+ saal aasaan se. Crown (upar ka artificial daant) 10-15 saal mein replace karna pad sakta hai. Lekin yeh sab aapki care pe depend karta hai — janein kaise.

📊 Realistic Lifespan — Comparison

🔩 Implant (Titanium Screw)25–Lifelong
👑 Implant Crown (Zirconia)15–20 saal
🌉 Dental Bridge10–15 saal
🦷 Denture (Partial)5–8 saal
🪨 Natural Tooth (Healthy)Lifetime
Research Says: 10 saal ki follow-up studies mein implant ki success rate 95-98% hai. Sahi care ke saath 30+ saal bhi chalte hain. Sweden mein kuch patients ke 40+ saal purane implants abhi bhi working condition mein hain.

✅ Lamba Chalane wale Factors vs ❌ Fail karne wale Factors

😁

Sahi Oral Hygiene

Roz brush + floss. Implant mein cavity nahi hoti lekin "Peri-implantitis" (gum infection) ho sakti hai — yahi sabse bada killer hai.

🚬

Smoking

Smokers mein implant failure rate 2-3x zyaada hai. Nicotine blood supply reduce karta hai — healing nahi hoti.

🏥

Regular Checkup

Har 6 mahine dentist ke paas jaao. Early signs agar hain toh turant pakad mein aa jaate hain.

🍬

Hard/Sticky Foods

Ice chabaana, hard nuts, chewy candy — crown pe extra stress dete hain. Crown damage ho sakta hai.

🩺

Diabetes Control

Controlled diabetes mein implant utna hi successful hote hain jitna non-diabetic mein. Uncontrolled mein risk zyaada hai.

😬

Teeth Grinding (Bruxism)

Raat ko daant pisna implant pe unnatural force deta hai. Night guard banana chahiye agar yeh problem hai.

🛡️ Implant ko Lamba Chalane ke 6 Rules

1

Special Implant Brush use karo

Interdental brush aur soft electric toothbrush implant ke aas-paas ke liye best hai. Regular brushing zaroor karein — implant mein cavity nahi lekin gum infection ho sakti hai.

2

Water Flosser sabse effective hai

Regular floss implant ke liye thoda tricky hota hai. Water flosser ya special implant floss use karo — yeh implant ke aas-paas ki jagah theek se saaf karta hai.

3

Hard cheezein mat chabao

Ice, hard candy, pen/pencil chewing, bottle caps khholna — yeh sab crown crack kar sakte hain. Zirconia crown strong hai lekin unbreakable nahi.

4

6 mahine mein ek baar checkup

Dentist implant ko examine karta hai — X-ray se bone level check karta hai. Agar bone loss shuru ho rahi hai toh early treatment se rok sakte hain.

5

Smoking band karo ya kam karo

Already placed implant mein smoking ka effect: healing slow, gum disease risk high, long-term failure rate 2x. Smoking quit karna implant ki life significantly badha deta hai.

6

Agar kuch unusual feel ho — wait mat karo

Implant ke aas-paas dard, sujan, ya looseness feel ho toh call karein. Early detection se usually implant save ho jaata hai.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Kya implant fail bhi ho sakta hai?
Haan, 2-5% cases mein implant fail hote hain. Failure ke 2 types hain: Early failure (3-4 mahine mein osseointegration nahi hua) aur Late failure (saalon baad infection ya bone loss se). Early failure mein re-implant ho sakta hai. Good news yeh hai ki 95%+ success rate hai aur modern implants bahut reliable hain.
Diabetic patients mein implant ho sakta hai?
Haan, ho sakta hai — lekin diabetes achhi tarah control mein hona chahiye (HbA1c 7.5% se kam). Controlled diabetes mein success rate normal patients jitni hi hai. Uncontrolled diabetes mein healing slow hoti hai aur infection risk badhta hai. Dentist se blood sugar report share karein — woh decide karenge.
Implant ke baad MRI ya X-ray ho sakta hai?
Haan, bilkul ho sakta hai. Modern titanium implants MRI-safe hain. X-ray, CT scan, aur regular dental X-rays bhi safe hain. Doctor ko sirf batana hai ki implant hai — woh protocol accordingly adjust karenge.
Implant mein dard hoga?
Surgery ke waqt anesthesia ki wajah se koi dard nahi hota. Surgery ke baad 3-5 din mild discomfort hota hai — prescribed medicines se manage hota hai. Zyaadatar patients report karte hain ki yeh extraction se zyaada painful nahi tha. Healing complete hone ke baad bilkul natural feel hota hai.
