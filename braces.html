What Top Orthodontists Recommend

📅 Best Age for Braces

Orthodontic treatment is most effective between 11–14 years when permanent teeth are erupted but bone is still adaptable. However, adults can get braces at any age with excellent results.

🦷 Retainers Are Forever

Teeth naturally want to drift back after braces. Research shows night-time retainer wear should continue indefinitely — at least 5+ years post-treatment for stable results. Skipping retainers = teeth shift back.

🧼 Hygiene During Braces

Braces create more food-trap surfaces. Use interdental brushes, water flossers, and fluoride mouthwash in addition to regular brushing. Poor hygiene during treatment can cause permanent white spot lesions.

⚠️ Foods to Avoid with Braces

Hard foods (nuts, raw carrots, ice), sticky foods (caramel, gum, toffee), and chewy foods (bagels, tough meat) can damage brackets and wires. Cut hard fruits into small pieces. See our braces food guide.
